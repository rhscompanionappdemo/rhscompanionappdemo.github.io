## Data Collection

This app collects only the data necessary to provide its services. We collect:

- Your name and student ID (stored locally)  
- Schedule information you provide  
- PLT session preferences  

All data is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to external servers.

---

## Third-Party Services

This app connects to your school's systems to fetch:

- News articles  
- PLT session listings  
- Calendar events  

These connections use your school's authentication and follow their privacy policies.

---

## Data Storage

All personal data is stored securely on your device using:

- SwiftData for schedule blocks  
- System File encryption for authentication tokens  
- Local cache for performance  

You can delete all data at any time through **Settings > Clear All User Data**.

---

## Permissions

This app may request permission to access:

- Camera — For scanning schedule documents  
- Calendar — For exporting schedule events  
- Notifications — For live activity updates  

All permissions are optional and you can manage them in iOS Settings.

---

## Zero-Data Upload Policy

We do **NOT**:

- Upload your personal information to our servers  
- Share your data with advertisers  
- Track your usage for analytics  
- Sell or rent your information  

This is a client-side app — your data stays on your device.

---

## Caches and Performance Data

The app uses local caches to improve performance. These caches may store:

- Cached calendar computations  
- PLT session data  
- Temporary web data  

These caches stay on your device and can be cleared in Settings.

---

## Retention of Data

Your personal data is retained on your device until you choose to delete it. Cached data is automatically managed by the app and may be cleared when it becomes stale or when you clear caches in Settings.
